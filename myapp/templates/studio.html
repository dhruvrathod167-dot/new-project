Architecture built on

ARCHAUS was founded on a single belief — that exceptional architecture demands exceptional people. Our studio brings together architects, designers, and thinkers who share an obsession with light, material, and the enduring power of honest space.

From our studio in Ahmedabad, we work across India and internationally — designing private residences, cultural institutions, and commercial landmarks that stand the test of time.

What Drives Us

Studio Values

I

Integrity

We say what we mean and build what we draw. No shortcuts, no compromises hidden in contracts.

C

Craft

Every detail — a joint, a reveal, a light fixture — receives the same attention as the building's form.

L

Longevity

We design for the next fifty years, not the next architectural trend cycle.

P

Partnership

Our clients are collaborators. The best buildings come from sustained, honest dialogue between designer and patron.
