Methodology

How a Building is Composed

We begin on the land itself — mapping sun path, prevailing wind, topography, and sightlines before a single wall is proposed. This analysis becomes the structural logic that every later decision answers to.

Volumes are sketched and tested in physical and digital models, weighing proportion against program. We iterate on massing until the silhouette earns its presence on the site.

Every junction is drawn before it is built. Stone-to-glass, timber-to-concrete — the honesty of a material is protected at the joint, never hidden behind trim.

Our team remains on site through completion, working alongside craftsmen to ensure the built result matches the precision of the drawing — down to the millimeter.
